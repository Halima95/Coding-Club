About The club

The main idea behind the Coding Club:

  • Be introduced to new technologies.

  • Vote on a certain tech to be the focus of the month.

  • During the month: share resources and help each other learn this tech.

  • Work on a project as a group or individually to be presented to each other by the end of the month as well as add it to our portfolio.
